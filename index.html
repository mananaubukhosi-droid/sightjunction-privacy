Privacy Policy for Sightjunction

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Sightjunction ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Sightjunction (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect several types of information from and about users of our App:

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Date of birth (to verify 18+ age requirement)
  • Profile photo
  • Bio and personal interests
  • Personality traits and pursuits

2.2 Location Data

  • Real-time GPS location (used only for proximity matching)
  • Your location is used to show you nearby users and display you to others
  • Your exact coordinates are never shared with other users

2.3 User-Generated Content

  • Photos (profile photo, OOTD photos)
  • Messages (text, voice, photos shared in chats)
  • Connection requests and interactions

2.4 Usage Data

  • App interactions and usage patterns
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Log data and crash reports

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality
  • Show you nearby users within your selected radius
  • Enable connections and communication between users
  • Improve user experience and App features
  • Ensure safety and prevent misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send important App notifications (not marketing)

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • User data: Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Photos: Cloudinary
  • All data is stored on secure cloud servers

4.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption
  • Secure Firebase authentication
  • Regular security updates
  • No storage of payment information

4.3 Data Retention

  • We retain your data while your account is active
  • You can delete your account at any time
  • Upon account deletion, your data is removed within 30 days

5. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in these circumstances:

5.1 With Other Users

  • Your profile information (name, bio, photos, interests) is visible to users you connect with
  • Your approximate location is shown to nearby users (exact coordinates are never shared)
  • Messages you send are visible to recipients

5.2 Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google) - user authentication and data storage
  • Cloudinary - image hosting and delivery

These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.

5.3 Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or to respond to legal process
  • To protect our rights, safety, or property
  • To prevent fraud or abuse

6. YOUR PRIVACY CONTROLS

You have control over your information:

6.1 Visibility Settings

  • Toggle visibility on/off (go invisible)
  • Control who can see you on the radar
  • Browse anonymously

6.2 Location Settings

  • Enable/disable location services at any time
  • Adjust scan radius (50m - 1km)
  • Location is only used when app is active

6.3 Data Access and Deletion

  • Access your data through profile settings
  • Edit or delete your profile information
  • Delete your account and all associated data

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Sightjunction is strictly for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a user under 18 has provided personal information, we will immediately delete their account and data.

8. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

These services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.
