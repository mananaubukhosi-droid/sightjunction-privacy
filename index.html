Sightjunction ("we," "our," or "the App") is a mobile application that helps people discover and connect with others nearby. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

By using Sightjunction, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Username and profile information
  • Date of birth (to verify you meet the minimum age requirement)
  • Photos you upload
  • Messages you send to other users
  • Support requests and communications with us

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data and interactions
  • Location data (only with your explicit permission)
  • Technical logs for debugging and performance

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Verify you meet the minimum age requirement
  • Enable you to discover and connect with nearby users
  • Send you notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve app performance and plan new features
  • Respond to support requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and age requirements
  • Detect and prevent violations of our policies

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored using Firebase, a service provided by Google LLC. Firebase uses industry-standard security practices. While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. You use the App at your own risk.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where legally required to retain it.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (Firebase, Cloudinary) to operate the App
  • Law enforcement if legally required
  • Other users — only information you choose to make public on your profile

6. Your Location Data

The App uses your location to show you nearby users. Location tracking requires your permission and can be disabled at any time in your device settings, though this will limit core app functionality. Background location updates may continue when the app is closed if you grant that permission.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed outside South Africa, including in the United States where Firebase servers are located. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to processing of your information

To exercise these rights, contact us at sightjunction.contact@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Sightjunction is intended for users 16 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16. If we discover an account belonging to a user under 16, we will immediately terminate the account and delete all associated data.

Users between the ages of 16 and 17 are permitted to use Sightjunction with the consent of a parent or legal guardian. By allowing a minor aged 16 or 17 to use the App, the parent or guardian agrees to this Privacy Policy on their behalf and takes responsibility for ensuring appropriate use.

How We Enforce Age Requirements

  • Date of birth is collected and verified at signup
  • Accounts for users under 16 are automatically blocked
  • Ongoing monitoring for suspicious activity indicating underage use
  • User reporting and investigation system
  • Immediate account termination upon discovery of underage users
  • Complete data deletion for terminated underage accounts

For Parents and Guardians

If you believe your child under 16 has created an account on Sightjunction, please contact us immediately:
