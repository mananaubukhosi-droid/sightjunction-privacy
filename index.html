9. Children's Privacy & Safety

Age Restriction

The App is only for users 18 years and older.

We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

Child Safety Standards

Sightjunction is committed to child safety:

  • Age Verification: Users must provide their date of birth during signup. Accounts are automatically rejected if the user is under 18.
  • No Content for Minors: The App is not designed for, marketed to, or intended to appeal to children or teenagers.
  • User-Generated Content: We do not knowingly allow minors to upload content. All user-generated content is subject to our Terms of Service.
  • Reporting: Users can report inappropriate content or suspicious accounts. We investigate all reports and take appropriate action.
  • Monitoring: We employ automated and manual moderation to detect and remove content that violates our policies.
  • No Advertisements to Children: We do not display advertisements targeted at children.
  • Parental Controls: While the App is not for minors, parents can contact us if they believe their child has created an account.

How We Protect Minors

  • Date of birth verification during account creation
  • Immediate account termination if a minor is discovered
  • Deletion of all data associated with underage accounts
  • Cooperation with parents and guardians
  • Compliance with child protection laws including COPPA and GDPR

If You Are a Parent or Guardian

If you believe your child under 18 has created an account on Sightjunction:

  1. Contact us immediately at sightjunction@gmail.com
  2. Provide proof of your relationship to the child
  3. We will verify and delete the account within 48 hours
