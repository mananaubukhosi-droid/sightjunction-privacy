1. Who We Are

Sightjunction is a mobile social discovery application that allows users to discover and connect with other users nearby using real-time proximity technology.

📧 Contact: sightjunction.contact@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name or username
  • Email address
  • Date of birth (to verify age)
  • Profile information (bio, profile photo)
  • Messages you send to other users
  • Support requests and communications with us

2.2 Location Information

Sightjunction uses location services to enable proximity features. We may collect:

  • Precise GPS location (real-time)
  • Approximate location based on device data
  • Location updates while the app is in use or in the background (if permitted)

📌 Location is used only to show users within your selected radius and enable proximity matching. We do not expose your exact coordinates publicly — we use proximity-based matching only. You can disable location access in your device settings, but core features will stop working.

2.3 Device & Technical Data

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • App crash logs and diagnostics

2.4 Usage Data

  • Features you interact with
  • Chat interactions
  • Connection requests sent or received
  • Time spent in the app

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Enable real-time nearby user discovery
  • Display users within your selected radius
  • Provide chat and connection features
  • Verify you meet the minimum age requirement
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Send notifications (if enabled)
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and fake accounts
  • Ensure safety and moderation
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Other Users

  • Your profile (username, bio, profile photo)
  • Your approximate proximity status within the radar
  • Only information you choose to make public

4.2 Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — authentication, database, cloud messaging
  • Cloudinary — photo hosting and delivery
  • Expo — app development infrastructure
  • Apple / Google services — platform services

These providers only process data to support app functionality.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose data if required by law, to comply with legal obligations, protect user safety, or prevent abuse and fraud.

5. Location Privacy

Sightjunction is a location-based application. Your location is used in real time to show nearby users. We do not permanently store precise location data. You can control location permissions through your device settings at any time. Background location updates may occur if you grant that permission.

6. Data Storage & Retention

  • Account data: stored until deleted by user
  • Location data: temporary, not permanently stored unless required for safety or fraud prevention
  • Chat data: stored while account is active
  • Inactive accounts may be deleted after a period of inactivity

7. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time through app settings or by contacting us. Upon deletion:

  • Your profile is removed
  • Personal data is deleted or anonymized within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained for legal or security reasons

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted communication (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase
  • Access controls to prevent unauthorized access
  • Regular system monitoring

However, no system is 100% secure. You use the App at your own risk.

9. Age Requirements & Young Users

Minimum Age

Sightjunction is intended for users 16 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16. If we discover an account belonging to a user under 16, we will immediately terminate the account and delete all associated data.

Users Aged 16–17

Users aged 16–17 are permitted to use Sightjunction with the consent of a parent or legal guardian. By allowing a minor aged 16–17 to use the App, the parent or guardian agrees to this Privacy Policy on their behalf.

How We Enforce Age Requirements

  • Date of birth collected and verified at signup
  • Accounts for users under 16 are automatically blocked
  • Ongoing monitoring for underage use
  • Immediate account termination upon discovery of underage users
  • Complete data deletion for terminated underage accounts

For Parents and Guardians

If you believe your child under 16 has created an account, contact us immediately:

  • Email sightjunction.contact@gmail.com with subject "Underage Account"
  • Provide the username or email of the account
  • We will verify and permanently delete the account within 48 hours
