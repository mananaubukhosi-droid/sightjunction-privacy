Privacy Policy for Sightjunction

Effective Date: 4 April 2026

Last Updated: 7 April 2026

Contact: sightjunction.contact@gmail.com

1. Introduction

Sightjunction (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a location-based social networking application. We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Username
  • Profile information (bio, interests, pursuits)
  • Email address (if provided)

2.2 Location Data

  • Exact GPS location
  • Real-time location (optional background mode)
  • Proximity radius (0–1000 meters)

Important: Exact distance between users is visible inside the app.

2.3 Device & Usage Data

  • Device type and operating system
  • App activity (profile views, scans, connections)
  • IP address

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To show nearby users based on location
  • To enable connections and chat between users
  • To display distance and proximity results
  • To improve app performance
  • To prevent abuse and ensure safety

4. User Visibility & Controls

  • Users can see nearby users by default
  • Visible profile data includes username, interests, and distance
  • Users can control visibility using a toggle in settings

5. Location Tracking

  • Location is used to calculate proximity between users
  • App may use background location if enabled by user
  • Users can disable location access in device settings

6. Chat & Interactions

  • Users can send connection requests and chat after matching
  • Messages are stored in Firebase database
  • Users can block and report other users

7. Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored using Firebase services
  • We do not use end-to-end encryption
  • Access to data is restricted to system functionality

No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell personal data
  • Data is shared with other users based on visibility settings
  • We may share data with Firebase (service provider)
  • We may disclose data if required by law

9. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Users can delete their account instantly in-app
  • Deleted data is removed from active systems immediately
  • Some backup data may remain for a limited time

10. Children’s Privacy

Sightjunction is intended for users aged 16 and older. Users aged 16–17 require parental consent. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 16 without consent.

11. Your Rights (POPIA)

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Contact: sightjunction.contact@gmail.com

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app or on our website.

13. Contact Us

Email: sightjunction.contact@gmail.com

14. Photo Library Access

Sightjunction may request access to your device’s photo library only when you choose to upload a profile picture or share content within the app.

  • Access is requested only when you actively select to upload an image
  • We only access photos that you specifically choose
  • We do not access your entire photo library

Uploaded images are used solely for profile display and social features within the app. We do not use your photos for advertising or share them with third parties without your consent.

You can deny or revoke photo access at any time through your device settings. However, this may limit your ability to upload images.
