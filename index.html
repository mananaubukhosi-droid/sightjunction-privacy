9. Children's Privacy & Safety Standards

Age Restriction

The App is strictly for users 18 years and older.

We do not knowingly collect, use, or share information from anyone under 18. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

Our Commitment to Child Safety

Sightjunction is committed to protecting children and complying with child safety laws including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR:

  • Age Verification: Users must provide their date of birth during account creation. If the user is under 18, account creation is automatically blocked.
  • Not Designed for Minors: The App is not designed for, marketed to, or intended to appeal to children or teenagers under 18.
  • No Marketing to Children: We do not advertise to, target, or market our App to anyone under 18.
  • User-Generated Content: We do not knowingly allow minors to upload photos, send messages, or create content.
  • Proactive Monitoring: We use both automated systems and manual review to detect and prevent underage users.
  • Reporting System: Users can report suspicious accounts or inappropriate content. We investigate all reports within 48 hours.
  • Immediate Action: Any account found to belong to a minor is immediately terminated and all associated data is deleted.

How We Enforce Age Restrictions

  • Date of birth collection and verification at signup
  • Automated rejection of accounts for users under 18
  • Ongoing monitoring for suspicious activity indicating underage use
  • User reporting and investigation system
  • Immediate account termination upon discovery of underage users
  • Complete data deletion for terminated underage accounts
  • Cooperation with parents, guardians, and authorities

Content Moderation

To maintain a safe environment suitable only for adults:

  • User-generated content is subject to our Terms of Service
  • Inappropriate content is removed promptly
  • Users can block and report other users
  • Violations result in warnings, suspensions, or permanent bans
  • We cooperate with law enforcement regarding illegal activity

For Parents and Guardians

If you believe your child under 18 has created an account on Sightjunction, please contact us immediately:

  1. Email us at sightjunction.contact@gmail.com with subject line "Underage Account"
  2. Provide the username or email address associated with the account
  3. Provide proof of your relationship to the child (if requested)
  4. We will verify and permanently delete the account within 48 hours
  5. All personal data will be removed from our systems

Third-Party Child Safety

Our third-party service providers (Firebase, Cloudinary) also comply with child protection laws. However, we are not responsible for their practices. Please review their privacy policies separately.
